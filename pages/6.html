Drought Freebies

Get your drought freebies here!

Show your commitment to water conservation.

The Water Authority offers several free promotional materials to residents and businesses who want to show support for the regional When in Drought outreach campaign. The items are given away at community events, Water Authority presentations and elsewhere.

Items include yard signs, magnetic car decals, fridge magnets, "Beer without water is very crunchy" and Drought Fighter coasters, hose nozzles, shower timers, leak detector tablets, table tents, counter signs, and buckets – all emblazoned with the When in Drought logo to help you share your commitment to water conservation. *All quantities may be limited.

Locations for Drought Freebies

 


Locations

Promotional items are available at the Water Authority headquarters, 4677 Overland Ave. in Kearny Mesa during regular business hours. To arrange a time to pick up your items, send an email to drought@sdcwa.org.
