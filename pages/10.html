Drought Information

A record low snowpack in the Sierra Nevada at the start of April 2015 and an executive order by the governor to reduce urban water use statewide show just how urgent water conservation is for San Diego County and the rest of California. California is now facing its fourth straight year of drought and we must all do what we can to save every day, every way.
